<p class="large">Our 17 practice rooms, all equipped with a piano, are ideal for Music students who need a private place for practice.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="section pattern-bg post no-padding-bottom"><!-- no padding-bottom class removes the padding-bottom from the post section -->
<div class="container">
<div class="row">
<div class="col-lg-12 post-body">
<p>As well as these rooms, students have access, with permission, to play the 21-stop 2-manual German organ in the Reid Concert Hall, and an experimental organ and 2-manual pipe organ both situated in Alison House. Harpsichords are also available.</p>
<p>In addition, limited special practice facilities are available to students who are percussionists, part of small ensembles, bagpipe players or players of amplified instruments.</p>

<p><span><span><span><span>We are currently organising the 2022-2023 Music Practice Room Passes. Please regularly check this page for updates.</span></span></span></span></p>
